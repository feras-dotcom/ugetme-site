Account and Data Deletion

We understand that you may wish to delete your ugetme account and associated data. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us at:

Email: techugetme@gmail.com

What happens when your account is deleted

  • Your user profile, chat history, and match data are permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Your email address and authentication credentials are removed from Firebase Authentication.
  • Your data is not recoverable after deletion is complete.
