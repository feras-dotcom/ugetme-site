Privacy Policy for ugetme

Last updated: October 30, 2025

1. Who we are

ugetme (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is a mobile application that helps users connect through shared interests and real-world activities.

2. Information we collect

  • Account data (name, email) via Firebase Authentication
  • Profile data (city, interests, optional phone number)
  • Usage and device data
  • Camera and photo library access for profile pictures
  • Push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

3. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with others based on shared interests
  • To send relevant notifications and updates
  • To improve app performance and user safety

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your data. We only share limited information with trusted services such as Firebase and Google APIs.

5. Data retention

We retain data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion by emailing us.

6. Your rights

You may access, update, or delete your personal data by contacting us at techugetme@gmail.com.

7. Children’s privacy

ugetme is for users aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Security

We use encryption, authentication, and secure storage, but no system is completely secure. Exercise caution when meeting in person.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be posted here.

10. Contact us
